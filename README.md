Autonomous Drone Challenge – ISRO Innovation R&D Open Challenge for Undergraduates (IROC-U) 2025
An onboard AI system is integrated into the drone to assist in visual boundary detection and autonomous decision-making for safe zone selection.

🔍 Features:
Square Boundary Detection:
Uses computer vision to detect square-shaped boundary markers on the ground (e.g., landing zone borders or no-fly zones).

Safe Spot Identification:
Analyzes the environment outside the detected boundary to locate open, flat, and obstacle-free regions suitable for emergency landing or rerouting.

Real-time Inference:
Runs lightweight AI models on the Raspberry Pi for real-time processing of camera feed.

💡 Purpose:
Enhances autonomous behavior in GPS-denied environments by enabling the drone to:

Avoid unsafe or restricted zones.

Dynamically select alternative paths or landing spots.

Make intelligent choices based on visual input.
